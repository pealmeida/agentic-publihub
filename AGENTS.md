## Learned User Preferences

- Align on technical strategy and produce foundational documentation before writing application code.
- Prefer switch/toggle controls instead of checkboxes for on/off settings.
- For hub editing flows (block picker, layout, integrations, widgets), use a bottom sheet on mobile and a modal on desktop.

## Learned Workspace Facts

- PubliHub is an agentic middleware and storefront for the Creator Economy: mobile-first creator Hubs, AI Copilot, and monetization via digital products, donations, and Quest-style gamified affiliate links.
- MVP open beta is Free and Starter only: no e-commerce or marketplace integration in scope; broader social integrations are phased as coming soon after core.
- Minimum balance to withdraw: R$ 100 on Free, R$ 50 on Starter.
- Open beta includes streaming browser-source / overlay widgets (e.g. OBS) so donations and fan interactions can appear on livestreams; a separate in-browser streamer HUD is not part of open beta.
